July

2024 Youth Night of Mango

July 14
Venue: Changsha - Helong Sports Center

A year ago, Mango TV, a Chinese video distribution service, and China Mobile, China’s largest mobile communications company, jointly launched the mobile phone monthly plan “Mango Card”. This concert to celebrate the first anniversary of the launch of the plan, and was broadcast live on Mango TV and MIGU Video, a subsidiary of China Mobile.
Hua Chenyu performed two songs this time. Of these, “Illusion and Reality (虚幻与现实)” was sung for the first time at an event other than his persnal concert.

  • “Light Up the Bonfire at the Galaxy’s End (点燃银河尽头的篝火)”

  • “Illusion and Reality (虚幻与现实)”

Official Video

















June

JD 618 Happy Night Gala

June 17
Venue: Changsha - Hunan International Convention & Exhibition Centre

JD, one of the biggest online direct retailer in China, holds an e-commerce sale called “618” around its founding day on June 18th annually.
This year, the day before 618, they held the “618 Happy Night Gala”, which is similar to the “Tmall Double 11 Gala”, held annually on the night before the biggest shopping festival in the world.
Hua Chenyu performed two songs this time. Of these, “Beauty Yet Revealed to Me (那些我尚未知道的美丽)” was sung for the first time at an event other than his persnal concert.

  • “Beauty Yet Revealed to Me (那些我尚未知道的美丽)”

  • “Let’s Go and Watch the Sunrise (走，一起去看日出吧)”

Official Video
















May

Monsoon Music Festival

Air date: May 18
Recording date: February 24
Venue: Macao - The City of Dreams

At the Monsoon Music Festival (季风音乐会) hosted by Hua Chenyu’s agency, a music program celebrating the 25th anniversary of Macau’s return to China, Hua sang a total of 6 songs.
Among them, Hua sang “Let’s Go and Watch the Sunrise (走，一起去看日出吧)” with Pax Kongo, who is also a fellow member of “Superboy 2013”.

  • “I Don’t Care (我管你)”

  • “Flight Mode (飞行模式)”

  • “I’m Here AKA I Really Want to Love This World (好想爱这个世界啊)）”

  • “Sea of Wind (风之海)”

  • “Extraordinary Ordinary Life (普通到不普通的人生)”

  • “Let’s Go and Watch the Sunrise (走，一起去看日出吧)”

Huya Live Star Ceremony - Huya Boom Night

April 27
Venue: Macao - Cotai Strip CotaiArena

“Huya Live”, China’s leader in game live streaming, held a “Huya Boom Night” live streaming gala in Macau on April 27. Hua Chenyu sang three songs at the gala.

Hunan TV Countdown Party
