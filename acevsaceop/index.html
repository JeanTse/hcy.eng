Ace VS Ace - Theme song of the variety show of the same name

In 2019, Hua Chenyu participated in the 4th season. of Zhejiang TV’s flagship variety show “Ace VS Ace (王牌对王牌 )” as a regular member. In the final episode, Hua sang his all-night arrangement of the show’s theme song with the show’s staff.
