September

“China Daily”

Interview: “A singer on a journey of discovery“

“China Daily”, an important window for the world to understand China, recently interviewed Hua Chenyu, a singer-songwriter who represents C-pop, and used a special page to report on Hua Chenyu’s Mars concert 2023.

“China Daily” is an English daily newspaper affiliated with the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Its main readers are diplomats, foreigners, tourists and Chinese people who want to improve their English. It has the widest print circulation of any English-language newspaper in China.

May

“X-MANGO” Inaugural Issue

In March 2022, Hua Chenyu, together with the talented female singer Tan Weiwei and the famous Chinese soprano Lei Jia, participated as a mentor in the music competition program “Flower Bloom in Spring (春天花会开)” aimed at promoting Chinese folk music. The three of them appeared on the cover of the magazine “X-MANGO”’s inaugural issue together.

“Blog world (博客天下)” Issue 354

The magazine has designed 2 covers to choose from, each of which comes with a desk calendar containing 6 cards.

An exclusive interview of over 10,000 words. It tells the behind-the-scenes story of Hua serving as chief director for the first time and successfully holding Mars Concert 2021, analyzing his emotional journey in the eight years since his debut, and demonstrating his attitude of insisting on self-transcendence as a singer.
