November

Tmall Double 11 Gala (Global Shopping Festival)

10, November
Venue: Shanghai Mercedes Benz Arena

Double 11 is also called Singles’ Day as anything with ’1’ in means single in China. It falls on November 11th annually (11-11 is the date with the most ’1’s). Started in 2009, it has become the biggest shopping festival in the world, similar to, but grossing several times more than, Black Friday in the USA and the Boxing Day and January sales in the UK.
And the Tmall Double 11 Gala, held the night before the shopping festival, has also become as popular as the Spring Festival Gala.
