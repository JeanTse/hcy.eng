Black and White Artist (黑白艺术家)

First released at “Mars Concert 2021” on December 3rd-5th, 2021. Officially released on December 20, 2022.
Be included in Hua Chenyu’s 5th album “Hope Forget”.

1. About the song

Hua Chenyu sharing his creative inspiration at Mars Concert 2021, before releasing this song for the first time

This year, I have written my new album.
And today’s new songs…How should I put it?
Actually, I feel that when I just started my career, I would often express my attitude towards life and music.
So, when I first started, I wrote songs like “I Don’t Care” and “Aliens”.
Because when I saw some negative comments on the internet, I just felt that: “If you don’t like to listen to me, then just shut your ears.”
I don’t care what you feel. Who cares?
At the beginning, I felt that I needed to refute them a bit. Isn’t it so?
Then some time after I started my career, I found: “Ha! This is a profession.” Isn’t that right?
Professional slanderers.
They absolutely won’t reason with you.
They will simply slander you.
When facing this kind of people, I understand that you don’t have to reason with them.
Therefore, I mentioned that this year, all songs on the new album are related to everyday life.
My first song, “Flight Mode”.
As I’ve introduced it, sometimes as I would chat with my friends.
Talk to strangers.
Sometimes, I would listen to their stories.
It seems that everybody is under high pressure.
So, I wrote “Flight Mode”.
It’s related to people around me.
“Flowers in a Small Town”, It’s about a friend of mine.
It’s about the guidance she has given to me, Which has allowed me to gain many understandings and insights.
And the third song is written for the anti-fans.
Now when I see those people who slander me on the internet, I won’t face them with the same attitude as in “I Don’t Care” and “Aliens”.
It’s because I have grown up.
When I see them like this, I will only say …
I won’t reason with them with an attitude.
I may even forgive your ignorance.
But I will laugh at you ten thousand times in my heart.
So, after I sing this song today, when you encounter those so-called “professional anti-fans” on the internet again,
Who purposefully incite fights online,
Who intentionally make people argue,
Please don’t argue with them on the internet.
You also don’t have to reason with them.
Just send this song to them and say: “Madam Old invites you to listen to a new song.” (Madam Old: Addresses oneself as a senior lady who is authoritative)
This song is called “Black and White Artist”.

Hua Chenyu Writing About His Creation of “Black and White Artist” on Weibo

This song is written for the anti-fans. My simple introduction to it is that when you meet keyboard knights or similar unreasonable people around you who like to babble, you just need to say to him “Ah yes yes yes yes yes, you are right. If you say the earth is shaped like a square, then it is a square. Come on baby, keep working hard! 😊”. That’s pretty much what it means. You just have to savor it yourself. I won’t introduce much more about the creation. After all, the anti-fans think they are the most professional in any field, you just need to listen to their bragging.
