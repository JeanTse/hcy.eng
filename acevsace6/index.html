“Ace VS Ace (王牌对王牌 )” Season 6 - Variety Show

From January to April 2021, Hua Chenyu participated in the 6th season of Zhejiang TV’s flagship variety show “Ace VS Ace (王牌对王牌)” as a regular member. Starting from Season 4 in 2019, Hua has starred in “Ace VS Ace” for three consecutive years.

1. About the show

“Ace VS Ace (王牌对王牌)”, the most popular variety show in China in recent years, is a large scale indoor sports TV show. In each episode several well-known guests such as singers, actress, and TV hosts are invited to participate in games and advertise their own products. Each episode has a different theme.

In addition to Hua Chenyu, the “Ace Family” that appears in every episode and competes with other guests, also includes Guan Xiaotong (actress), Jia Ling (comedian) and Shen Teng (comedian who participated in the same travel reality show “Flowers on Trip” with Hua). Starting from this season, in addition to the fixed host Shen Tao, Song Yaxuan, a member of the CPOP Boy Group TNT, has also been invited to serve as the host assistant.

Hua Chenyu is in charge of music in the “Ace Family”, and in the talent show segment, Hua usually covers songs related to the theme of this episode. He can also achieve excellent results in music-related games.
